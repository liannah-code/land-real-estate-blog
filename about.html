Welcome!

Hi there! I'm glad you're here. I started this blog to help people—especially Kenyans at home and abroad—navigate the often confusing process of buying land and real estate in Kenya.

Why I Started This Blog

Like many, I struggled to find clear, trustworthy, and up-to-date information when I was first looking to invest in property. This blog is my way of sharing everything I’ve learned—and continue to learn—on the journey.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Step-by-step guides on buying land and houses
  • Tips for avoiding scams
  • Advice tailored for the diaspora
  • Insights on investment locations
  • Helpful resources like checklists, contracts, and more
