Why Mombasa?

Mombasa offers prime beachside and tourism-related land investment opportunities. It's also growing in residential development due to demand from locals and diaspora buyers.

Popular Areas in Mombasa:

  • Nyali
  • Bamburi
  • Likoni
  • Shanzu

Tips for Buying Land in Mombasa

Due to its coastal location, always check for land ownership history (freehold vs leasehold), ensure beach access rights, and confirm survey boundaries.
